MEDITATION


“Mediation is not about controlling your thoughts , but to stop letting them control you”

Our meditation sessions are a students date with themselves. Where they realise they are so much more inside than what is visible on the outside.

Course Duration: 3 Months (Once a week)

YOGA


"Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self." -- The Bhagavad Gita"

Yoga provides one of the best means of self improvement and attaining one’s full potential.According to Yogic Scriptures the practice of Yoga leads to the union of individual consciousness with the universal consciousness.

PRANAYAM


“Anybody can breath therefore anybody can practice pranayam”

The word Pranayam comes from Sanskrit meaning “extension of life force” . This exercise does just that , fews minutes into the exercise and you will feel the rejuvenating energy coursing through you .

MUSIC THERAPY


Modern science and medicine are now rediscovering the healing powers of music. And music therapy -- the specialized use of music in treating persons with special needs in mental and physical health, rehabilitation and special education -- is gaining ground. In the West it is now an accepted form of treatment even within orthodox medical practice. We fused this music therapy with yoga for a rich and giving experience.
